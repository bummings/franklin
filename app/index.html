    Anthony “Sailor Tony” Reilly is the proprietor of Franklin's Barber Shop, located in the historic Fishtown neighborhood of Philadelphia.

    A Philadelphia native, Sailor Tony travelled extensively to hone his craft before coming back to his hometown to put down roots and put into practice what he had learned in his travels.

    After college, he joined the Navy as a Hospital Corpsman, and was deployed to Haiti and Afghanistan. During his 5 years in the Navy, Sailor Tony developed and pursued an interest in traditional barbering. On the G.I. Bill, he completed Barber School and was recruited to a Shop Manager role at Alfie's Barber Shop in Melbourne, Florida.

    It is with great care, attention, and pride in the ancient craft of barbering, that Sailor Tony returns to his native Philadelphia to help people “look better, feel better", as the old barber slogan goes. When you pay a visit to Franklin's Barber Shop you will be supporting a veteran and a traditional, American craftsman at work.
